MILESTONE

The first event of Induce that shared a platform for all to share their views on Inventive, Innovative & Interrogative skills.
We conducted Milestone event to be an National Level Essay competition in association with sponsership from Publisuthra & publicity partners Engineering Principles .

Essay to be submitted is given with no restrictions for expressing their thoughts.
To make it avail by all, we planned this event for 10 days.
It was a challenging event for all where only Top 10 essay submissions were offered with Internship program from Publisuthra.
Around 200 participants were participated in this event.
